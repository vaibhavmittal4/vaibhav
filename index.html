What is child cancer?
Child cancer, or pediatric cancer, is a complex and devastating disease affecting children. Here are some key points about child cancer:
Definition: Child cancer refers to the development of malignant tumors in children under the age of 18.
Types: It encompasses various types of cancer, including leukemia brain tumors, neuroblastoma, Wilms tumor, retinoblastoma, lymphomas, and sarcomas.
